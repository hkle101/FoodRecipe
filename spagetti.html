Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs and mix with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Combine spaghetti with pancetta and remove from heat.
  5. Quickly mix in egg and cheese mixture and serve immediately.
