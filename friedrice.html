Chicken Fried Rice

A quick and delicious one-pan dinner you can make in under 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (day-old preferred)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, diced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (optional)
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan and scramble the eggs. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add cooked chicken and stir well.
  4. Add the cooked rice and mix everything together.
  5. Pour soy sauce over the mixture and stir to combine.
  6. Add scrambled eggs back into the pan.
  7. Stir in green onions, season with salt & pepper, then serve warm.
