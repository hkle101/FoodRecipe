  • Spaghetti Carbonara
  • Chocolate Cake
  • Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 100g cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • 50g butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and cocoa powder in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and melted butter and mix well.
  4. Pour into a greased baking pan and bake for 25–30 minutes.
  5. Let cool before serving.
